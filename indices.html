About These Indices

Each index on this page is a structured simplification of established academic frameworks - drawn from peer-reviewed research in psychology, identity development, cross-cultural theory, and behavioral science. The full references are in the corresponding books.

These are not clinical instruments. They have not been independently validated in a research trial. They are not diagnostic tools. What they are: carefully designed reflective assessments that translate complex academic constructs into accessible, scoreable language - so that the ideas explored in the Smudged Edges of Self series do not stay theoretical.

The gap this fills is real. Academic psychology is rigorous but inaccessible to most people. Self-help is accessible but rarely grounded in science. No existing platform combines book-grounded indices, cross-index profiling, and an AI companion designed around the same conceptual framework. That combination is what Living Literature is building.

Rayan B. Vasse holds a PhD in biological sciences and a diploma in software engineering, with over 35 years across biotechnology, international environments, and cross-cultural life. The Smudged Edges of Self series emerged from that intersection - not from a single discipline, but from the specific combination of scientific methodology, lived multicultural experience, and systems thinking that building something like this actually requires.

Use these indices as what they are: a mirror, not a verdict.

Urban Monasticism

STI - Solitude Tolerance Index (limited demo)

-

Solitude is a trainable state. STI measures how well you function when you are alone.

Rate each statement 1 (not true) to 5 (fully true).

I can sit in silence for 10 minutes without distress.
I feel like a full version of myself even when no one is watching.
I do not need social feedback to know who I am.

Persona Continuum

PFI - Persona Fluidity Index (limited demo)

-

PFI maps how you shift between roles: adaptation, fatigue, integration, and digital performance load.

Rate each statement 1 (strongly disagree) to 5 (strongly agree).

Adaptive Fluency: Reinvention feels more like freedom than anxiety.
Performance Fatigue: I have felt identity depletion rather than simple overwork.
Integration Capacity: I can discuss my different selves without fragmentation.
Digital Persona Load: I feel pressure to perform an identity online.

Full PFI in Persona Continuum.

Fourth Culture

CCI - Cross-Cultural Index (limited demo)

-

CCI maps cultural layering through exposure and lived psychological impact.

Two factual and two experiential questions.

Countries lived in for at least one year?
Fluent languages?
No single place fully feels like home?
Performing one identity here and another elsewhere?

Full CCI in Fourth Culture.

Belonging Beyond Tribes

BTI - Beyond Tribe Index (limited demo)

-

BTI asks what belonging has cost you, and how you balance connection with self-preservation.

Rate each statement 1 (strongly disagree) to 5 (strongly agree).

I suppress opinions or feelings to stay accepted in a group.
I can connect deeply without needing to fully belong.
Fitting in has required becoming someone I did not fully recognize.
I know which relationships expand me and which shrink me.

Full BTI is in final preparation for Belonging Beyond Tribes (limited demo).
