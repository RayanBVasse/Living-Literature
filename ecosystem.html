Living Literature: Where books do not end

Living Literature is the interactive reading layer of the ecosystem. Reading continues through AI companions, reflective continuity, and longitudinal return.

  • Bookshelf continuity: a book becomes an ongoing dialogue system
  • Companion modes: Prometheus (analytical) and Selene (reflective)
  • Reader trajectory: interaction across sessions, not one-off chat

Reflective Indices: Partial IPR

The indices in this series are not invented frameworks. They are careful simplifications of decades of academic research in psychology, identity theory, neuroscience, and sociology - translated into questions a person can actually answer about their real life. The references that built them are in each book. The simplification is the point.
