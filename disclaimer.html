Reflective tools - not assessments

The indices and frameworks presented in this ecosystem - including the Solitude Tolerance Inventory (STI), Persona Fluidity Inventory (PFI), Cross-Cultural Inventory (CCI), Bonding Tribes Inventory (BTI), Traits and Orientations Inventory (TOI), and Integrated Persona Reflection (IPR) - are reflective inventories designed to deepen self-understanding and support personal dialogue. They are conversation starters, not diagnostic instruments.

These tools have not been submitted to psychometric validation procedures. They do not produce clinically reliable scores. They do not diagnose, classify, or predict behaviour. Numerical or categorical outputs are illustrative scaffolding, not scientific measurements.

No reader should make significant personal, medical, relationship, or career decisions based solely on these tools.

AI interactions

The AI companion interactions on this platform (Prometheus, Selene) are powered by large language model technology. They are not conscious, do not understand you as an individual, and do not retain information between sessions. Responses that feel personally resonant are a function of language pattern - not genuine knowledge of your situation.
